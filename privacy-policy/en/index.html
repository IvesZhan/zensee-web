ZenSee Privacy Policy

Welcome to ZenSee. We value your privacy and want you to understand clearly: what information we collect when you use this application, how this information is used, and how you can manage your own data.

ZenSee is provided and operated by the individual developer, Zhan Yu.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: e.g., email address, login session information, used to complete registration, login, and identification.
  • Usage Data: e.g., meditation duration, mood records, check-in times, group interaction records, used to provide recording, statistics, and synchronization functions.
  • Preferences: e.g., reminder times, sound effect toggles, interface preferences, used to save your personalized settings.
  • Device and Log Information: used for troubleshooting, analyzing stability, and improving service quality.

2. How We Use Information

  • Providing and maintaining core functions such as account login, data synchronization, meditation records, and statistical displays.
  • Sending you local or remote notifications after you actively enable reminders.
  • Improving product experience and service quality, such as fault analysis, performance optimization, and functional adjustments.
  • Fulfilling necessary obligations when required by laws, regulations, or platform rules.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

We use Supabase as our backend service provider for data storage, account authentication, and API services. Third parties process data only within the scope necessary to provide relevant technical services.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent;
  • As necessary to achieve core service functions;
  • As required by laws, regulations, regulatory, or judicial authorities.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data, including transmission encryption, access control, and the principle of least privilege. However, no method of internet transmission can promise absolute security; please understand and properly keep your own account information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • During your use of this application, we retain the data necessary to provide the service.
  • You can request to delete your account and related data through the "Delete Account" option in the application.
  • Where laws and regulations provide otherwise, such provisions shall prevail.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and you can also withdraw certain authorizations, such as turning off notification permissions.

8. Minors

If the laws of your region require minors to use relevant services with the consent of a guardian, please use this application under the guidance of a guardian.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy according to business changes or legal requirements. The updated version will be published on this page and will take effect from the date of publication.
